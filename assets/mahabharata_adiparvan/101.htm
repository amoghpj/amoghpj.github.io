4

5

6

ājagāma tato 'paśyaṃs tam ṛṣiṃ taskarānugāḥ
7

tena gacchāmahe brahman pathā śīghrataraṃ vayam
8

9

10

saṃyamyainaṃ tato rājñe dasyūṃś caiva nyavedayan
11

12

13

nirāhāro 'pi viprarṣir maraṇaṃ nābhyupāgamat

14

15

16

17

18

19
